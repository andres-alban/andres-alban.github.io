Andres Alban

Ph.D. Candidate

INSEAD, Technology and Operations Management area

Contact: andres.alban at insead edu

I am Ph.D. candidate in the Technology and Operations Management area at INSEAD. My primary research interest is the use of stochastic simulation and optimization to improve healthcare processes. My thesis is about designing optimal response-adaptive clinical trials.

I received my undergraduate degree in applied mathematics and physics from the New Jersey Institute of Technology. During my Ph.D. studies I participated in the European Sepsis Academy (ESA) where I completed coursework related to sepsis and participated in medical research at the Amsterdam Medical Center and Attikon hospital in Athens.
